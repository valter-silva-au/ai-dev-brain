# Customer Interview Framework (Mom Test)

> Idea-stage worksheet. Interview to learn about the customer's *life and past
> behaviour* — never to pitch or to ask them to predict the future. Run the
> question list through `adb initiative lint-interview <file>` to catch
> hypothetical / leading / opinion questions before you use it.

## Rules (The Mom Test)
1. Talk about **their life**, not your idea.
2. Ask about **specifics in the past**, not generics or the future.
3. **Talk less, listen more.**

## Questions (past-tense, non-leading)
> Good: "Tell me about the last time you…", "How do you currently…", "What did
> you do when…", "How much did that cost you?", "Walk me through…".
> Bad (the linter flags these): "Would you use…", "Do you think…", "Would you pay…",
> "Don't you hate it when…".

- TODO — "Tell me about the last time you [hit the problem]. What happened?"
- TODO — "How do you handle [job] today? Walk me through the last time."
- TODO — "What did that cost you (time/money)? What did you try before?"
- TODO — "What else have you tried? Why did you stop?"
