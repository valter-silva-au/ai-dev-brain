## Legend
- **Type:** `fact` = observed past behaviour or a real transaction · `claim` = something
  someone said they'd do (discount heavily) · `metric` = a measured number.
- **Strength:** `strong` = past behaviour, money, retained usage · `weak` =
  hypothetical, opinion, a single warm intro.
