## Network effects (NFX taxonomy)

- **Type** (direct / marketplace / data / platform / social)? Which side, and why
  does each added node increase value for the others?
- **Defensibility over time** — does the effect strengthen or decay as you scale?

## Switching costs (the stickiness audit)

- What does a customer *lose* by leaving (data, integrations, workflow, learning)?
- How deep are the integrations (see `adb ingest` / connectors)? Rank shallow→deep.
- What would a competitor have to rebuild to win the account back?

## a16z / durability qualifiers

- Is the advantage **accumulating** (gets stronger with usage/time) or static?
- Is it **proprietary** (hard to buy/copy) vs. merely a head start?
