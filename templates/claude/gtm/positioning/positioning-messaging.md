## Positioning (competitive alternatives → unique value)

- **Competitive alternatives.** What would customers use if you didn't exist?
- **Unique attributes.** What do you have that the alternatives don't?
- **Value (& proof).** What can customers do because of those attributes? Evidence?
- **Target customers.** Who cares a lot about that value?
- **Market category.** What context makes your value obvious?
- **Trend (optional).** What relevant trend makes you more relevant now?
