Binding conduct for this worktree. A worktree is a checkout of another repo whose
own CLAUDE.md shadows the KB root, so these do NOT auto-load — they are inlined here.

- **Reply as the project owner, never as the AI.** Outward text (PRs, comments, commits) is
  first-person in the owner's voice. No AI signatures, "Generated by", or co-author trailers.
- **Branch discipline.** Work only in THIS worktree on THIS branch. The workspace root
  (`$ADB_HOME`) stays on `main`; `repos/` clones are read-only. New branch work = a new adb task.
- **Production safety.** No destructive or outward action (delete/terminate/modify prod,
  push, PR, deploy, send) without explicit confirmation. Prefer read-only; assume prod when unsure.
- **Verify the model.** Confirm the active model from your own injected model line, not the
  settings/status bar (some proxies silently downgrade). If it is not the expected model, STOP and confirm before continuing.
- **Conventional Commits** for branch, commit, and PR-title naming. Keep replies short; ask when unsure.
